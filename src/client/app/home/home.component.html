Model papers is useful when you want to try out whole paper. You can simply select you prefered district, year and subject you like to try out. You will be given whole paper with pre-allocated time as examiner allocate

Time Based Questions

Time Based Question can be used when you limited time and want to try out some questions. Simply select the subject and enter time you want and system will automatically select questions according to your preferences
